Thomas McLean

Author | Poet

Thomas McLean is a young writer of fiction, poetry and nonfiction, currently living and practicing in Naarm. Once refered to as talented in a rather offhand manner, he's been chasing the same high ever since. He's yet to reach it. If he was known for anything at all, then he'd like to think it would be for his sick fashion sense and sophisticated music taste, but, bar that, being known for writing cool stuff that people like would be good too.

When he's not writing, Thomas likes puzzling over worryingly simple brain teasers and trying to forget that he's going to die someday.
